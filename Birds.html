Characteristics of Birds

  1. Feathers.
  2. High metabolism.
  3. A four-chambered heart.
  4. A beak with no teeth.
  5. A lightweight but strong skeleton.
  6. Production of hard-shelled eggs.

Hyacinth Macaw

  2. While macaws range drastically, in terms of size, they are the world's largest parrots.
  3. Macaws can live for a long time.
  4. They can mimic human speech.
